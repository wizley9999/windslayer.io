# Gem Rules for Weapon Reinforcement

When enhancing a weapon, gem properties **can cancel each other out** if they are opposites. Only the net property effect remains.

## Opposite Properties

| Property | Opposite Property |
| -------- | ----------------- |
| Fire     | Water             |
| Earth    | Wind              |

**Example:**

- Adding **3 Fire gems** and **2 Water gems** results in **+1 Fire property**.  
  (Because Water cancels out Fire.)

---

## Effect on Skills

A weapon with applied properties **enhances all your skills of that property**, except for the opposite property.

**Examples:**

1. Weapon with **+24 Fire**: Fire-based skills like _Firebolt_ or _Meteor_ are enhanced, but Icebolt is not.
2. Weapon with **+8 Wind**: Wind-based skills like _Wind Cutter_ are enhanced, but Meteor is not.

---

## Property Effects Against Monsters

When monsters share the same property, **only skills of the same property are enhanced**.

**Examples:**

- In **Serien** (many Water property monsters), a **Water weapon** only enhances Icebolt.
- In **Seren Meadow** (many Wind property monsters), a **Wind weapon** only enhances Wind Cutter.
- In **Serien**, with many Water monsters:
  - **Fire weapon** → Firebolt and Meteor are enhanced
  - **Earth weapon** → Firebolt and Meteor are enhanced

> Note: Skills of a property unrelated to the monster property are **not enhanced**.
