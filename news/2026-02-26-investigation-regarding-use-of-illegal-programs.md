# [Notice] Investigation Regarding Use of Illegal Programs (Hacks) and Distribution of Illegitimate In-Game Currency

Hello,
This is the WindSlayer Team.

Recently, through multiple reports and internal monitoring, we have identified suspected cases involving the use of illegal programs (hacks) and the creation and distribution of illegitimately generated in-game currency (gold).

We take this matter very seriously and are currently conducting a thorough investigation based on all relevant data, including login records, combat logs, and transaction histories of the accounts involved.

If the investigation confirms the use of illegal programs, the relevant accounts will be **permanently suspended** without prior warning.

In addition, accounts that have purchased or received illegally generated currency may also be subject to strict sanctions, including **permanent or long-term suspension**, regardless of intent.

Please be clearly advised that acquiring in-game currency of unknown origin through player-to-player transactions may be considered a violation of our operational policies, even if you have not directly used any illegal programs.

The Operations Team will not tolerate any actions that undermine the fairness of the game, and we may implement mass sanctions and additional measures if necessary.

We strongly urge all players not to engage in player-to-player gold transactions and to report any suspicious activity immediately through Customer Support.

We will continue to take firm action to maintain a fair and healthy game environment.

Thank you.

WindSlayer Team
